4.Californication - Red Hot Chili Peppers (1999)

This album marked the return of the Californian band's iconic guitarist John Frusciante from a drug addiction that nearly resulted in his death. From introspective songs such as "Otherside" and "Parallel Universe" where singer Anthony Kiedis reflects on his addiction and his past choices, to the melancholic title-track which reflects on the perverse culture of Hollywood and the Spanish guitar-tinged "Road Trippin", a collection of songs 333with a very wide range of influences from an iconic band in its most classic incarnation.

Standout Tracks
